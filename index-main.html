Discover Ireland

With so much to see, do and explore.... why wait?!

Learn all about the isle of Ireland, including it's history, not-to-be-missed sightseeing, and savoury food & drink!

Our hope is that we can inspire curiosity and adventure for our readers, and provide some helpful information for those considering visiting the emerald isle one day!

Many people don't realise that the island of Ireland is actually split into two countries. Northern Ireland, commonly referred to as the North, and the Republic of Ireland, referred to as the Republic. The North, which is part of the United Kingdom, is comprised of 6 counties, whereas the Republic, which includes 26 counties, remains part of the European Union. This is an important distinction when visiting Ireland, and you will learn more in our section on the "troubles."
