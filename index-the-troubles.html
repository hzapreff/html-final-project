The Troubles

It's hard to imagine such a picturesque landscape facing the adversity that Ireland has, but in fact, Irish have been divisive for for over a hundred years, with violence increasing since the "troubles" began in the 1960's. The article Conflict in Europe: The Troubles in Ireland does a wonderful job of outlining the difficulties in Ireland, which will prepare you for the culture and heritage you are sure to learn about on your trip.In fact, the impact of Bloody Sunday in 1972 was so impactful, U2 wrote a song about it. If you are every in Belfast, make sure you take a taxi tour of the murals that have been painted to recognize the lives lost and struggles faced by the Irish people.

Fun fact: Bono is actually from Ireland!
